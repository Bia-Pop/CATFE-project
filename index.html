About Us

Welcome to Catfe, the purrfect blend of coffee, comfort, and companionship! At Catfe, we believe in creating a space where cat lovers and curious coffee enthusiasts can unwind, enjoy delicious drinks and treats, and spend quality time with our adorable, adoptable feline friends.

Whether you're here to sip on a latte, savor a freshly baked pastry, or study for your next exam, our cozy cafe is designed to make every visit memorable. 

Come for the coffee, stay for the cats.

Q&A

Do I need a reservation to visit Catfé?

Reservations are recommended, especially during busy times, but walk-ins are welcome when space allows. Contact us via email or phone for a reservation. 

Can I bring my own cat to Catfé?

For the safety and comfort of our resident cats, outside pets are not allowed at Catfé. Tell your cat/cats pspsps from us! 

Are the cats adoptable?

Our cats are permanent residents of Catfé and are not available for adoption. 

Is there an age limit for visiting?

Children are welcome but must be accompanied by an adult at all times. We recommend a calm and respectful approach to ensure everyone, including the cats, has a pleasant experience.

Can I volunteer or donate to support the cats?

While we don’t offer a volunteer program, donations for our cats' care are always welcome. You can also support us by visiting often and spreading the word!

What precautions are taken for allergies?

If you have mild allergies, you’re welcome to enjoy the café area without entering the cat lounge. For more severe allergies, we recommend consulting with a doctor before visiting.
